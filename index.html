About Me

I am a science teacher with 7 years of teaching experience and over 10 years of experience working with students of different levels. I received my education at the University of Texas at San Antonio in multiple fields of science. Throughout my career as an educator, I have focused on the utilization of technology resources in the delivery of science content. This has included online simulations, virtual labs, and data analysis in the fields of earth and space science. While exploring these new ways of engaging students in science, I also trained other educators at both the district and state level in the new techniques I was learning.
